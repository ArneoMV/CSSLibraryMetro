Collapse

Collapse is a collapsible component that is used when you want to display or hide a large amount of content. Allows easy display of content at the touch of a button. It serves to rid the page of redundant content and give it a modern minimalist look.

    It is useful for:

  • grouping content
  • displaying long lists
  • displaying hidden data and more
