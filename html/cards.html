Card's

The cards serve to display larger amounts of data within a smaller whole. They have become popular over the last few years as cards are the perfect response to the rise of responsive design. Cards are standalone elements that can be combined with different content. They respond easily to different screen sizes.

There are three types of cards that we can use in future application development; profile card, product card and portfolio card. When implementing a card within the app, we need to follow the guidelines for a better user experience:

  • Too many visible cards at one time can create a problem. Too many tabs on the screen can become overwhelming for the user when scanning content.
  • Optimum number of actions possible within the card. Making every part of the card interactive can be confusing for the user
  • Avoid overloading the card with unnecessary content and illustrations. We split the tabs into three sections that represent primary, secondary, and tertiary content to allow users to focus on content that interests them
